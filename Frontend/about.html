About CodeProgramix

CodeProgramix is a comprehensive platform dedicated to supporting engineering students throughout their academic journey and beyond. Our mission is to provide a one-stop solution for all your engineering education needs, from study materials to real-world opportunities.

Founded by Sonu Kumar, a 4th-year Bachelor of Technology student from Meerut Institute of Technology, CodeProgramix is aimed at bridging the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. Sonu, with a deep passion for education and engineering, created this platform to help fellow students navigate their academic and professional paths effectively.

Our Vision

To create a global community of skilled and knowledgeable engineers, equipped with the tools and resources they need to succeed in their careers and make a positive impact on the world.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Curated study materials covering all major engineering disciplines
  • Partnerships with leading companies to provide exclusive internship opportunities
  • Regular webinars and workshops conducted by industry experts
  • A supportive community of students, educators, and professionals

About the Founder

CodeProgramix was created by Sonu Kumar, a passionate 4th-year Bachelor of Technology student at Meerut Institute of Technology. Sonu has a vision to empower students with the knowledge and skills required to succeed in their engineering careers. His drive to build this platform stems from his own academic experiences and his desire to make learning more accessible to everyone.
